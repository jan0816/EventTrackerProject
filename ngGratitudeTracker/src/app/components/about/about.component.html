About

The purpose of our gratitude tracker project stems from a hippie and her even more hip dog wanting to make the most of life and not forget the little things in life that we are grateful for. It's easy to keep wanting more in life but like Aesop says, gratitude turns what we have into enough. This replaces a physical gratitude journal and we want to be able to track 3 daily gratitudes to remind us of what truly matters most in life.
